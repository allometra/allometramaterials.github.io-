2. What Data We Collect

This website collects limited data only when you provide it or consent to analytics. Specifically:

  • Analytics data (with consent): If you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized usage data, including pages visited, time on site, approximate geographic location (country/city level), device type, and referral source. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Contact form data: If you submit a contact or inquiry form, we collect your name, email address, and message content in order to respond to you.
  • No analytics without consent: If you decline cookies, Google Analytics fires in anonymous/cookieless mode. No personal identifiers or tracking cookies are stored.

3. Cookies

We use the following cookies on this site:

  • _ga, _ga_E58NT3LMFN (Google Analytics): Set only if you accept analytics cookies. Used to distinguish users and sessions for aggregate traffic analysis. Expires after 2 years.
  • cookie_consent (Functional): Stores your cookie preference (accepted or declined) so we do not ask again on repeat visits. This is a strictly necessary functional cookie and does not require consent.

We do not use advertising, retargeting, or social media cookies.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For EU/EEA visitors, our legal basis for processing personal data is:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Analytics data is processed only with your explicit consent, which you can withdraw at any time below.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Contact form responses are processed to respond to your inquiry.

5. How We Use Your Data

  • To understand aggregate website traffic and improve our site
  • To respond to inquiries submitted via contact forms
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

Under GDPR, EU/EEA residents have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@allometramaterials.com.
